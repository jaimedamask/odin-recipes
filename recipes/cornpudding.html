Jiffy Corn Casserole

"Best thanksgiving side dish ever!! Corn Casserole" by adinaplus is licensed under CC BY 2.0 .

Corn casserole, or corn pudding, is a quick and easy dish for a variety of occasions. It's perfect as a side dish for holidays, family dinners, or anytime you need comfort food. It has the perfect blend of sweet and savory, and hands-on cooking time is minimal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 stick butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 (14.75oz) can cream-style corn
  • 1 cup corn kernels, fresh or frozen (thawed)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 (8.5oz) box Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 1.5-quart casserole dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter, eggs, and sour cream. Stir in the cream-style corn and corn kernels, followed by the salt and corn muffin mix. Stir until well combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 40-55 minutes, or until golden brown and the center is set.
