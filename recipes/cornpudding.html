Grandma's Corn Pudding

Ingredients

  • 5 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 (15.25oz) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 2 (14.75oz) cans cream-style corn

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 2-quart casserole dish.
  2. Whisk eggs lightly in a large bowl. Add milk, melted butter, sugar, and cornstarch; whisk until well combined. Stir in drained corn and cream-style corn until fully blended. Pour mixture into the prepared casserole dish.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown (about 1 hour).
